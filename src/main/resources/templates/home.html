Extreme Ultra

Hardanger Jøkulen 2020

Hardanger Jøkulen Ultra is an ultra marathon quite unlike any other! Taking place among the beautiful mountains and fjords of Hardanger
the race combines a hardcore mountain climb of a marathon with the best views of Norwegian nature.

  • 100km with 3200 climbing meters
  • Beautiful vistas along the hike
  • Hot food at Finse
  • Feet and body checkups along the way
  • Personal that walk the route and make sure everyone is healthy and well
